Research Papers

Below are the research papers that I am currently working on.

Multi-Modal Meta Learner (M3L): A Unified, Scalable Architecture for State-of-the-Art Image Processing

Abstract:A state-of-the-art image processing model that unifies several advanced deep learning techniques into a single architecture. It integrates Sparse CNN, Vision Transformers, Graph Neural Networks, along with Diffusion Models, Neural Architecture Search, and Masked Autoencoders for self-supervised representation learning.

Work in Progress

A Lightweight Deep Learning Framework for Detecting Potato Disease Using the Grasshopper Optimization Algorithm

Abstract:This study focused on artificial intelligence applied in potato disease detection,through a lightweight deep learning framework. The suggested model uses an integration of MobileNetV2 with Grasshopper Optimization Algorithm,for five potato diseases for classification.

Work in Progress

Deep Learning-Based Brain Tumor Identification

Abstract: This research paper mainly focuses on using deep learning techniques, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to identify brain tumors from magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) scans. The chosen dataset contains scans of brain tumors consisting of 4 classes.
